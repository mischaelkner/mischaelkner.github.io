Welcome to my website! I am a second year PhD student in the Hausel Group at the Institute of Science an Technology Austria.

Before starting my PhD, I studied mathematics at the University of Vienna. There, my BSc thesis was supervised by Andreas Cap and my MSc thesis by Shantanu Dave.

I am interested in algebraic and differential geometry and their interplay with the structure and representation theory of Lie groups. My PhD project is in geometric representation theory, with a focus on natural representation-theoretic symmetries. Using the recently introduced big algebras, I am trying to study such symmetries geometrically.

Research

Though different in the details, this experience in noncommutative geometry has been helpful for work in algebraic geometry.
