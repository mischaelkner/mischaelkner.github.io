Welcome to my website! I am a second year PhD student in the Hausel Group at the Institute of Science and Technology Austria. My e-mail address is mischa[dot]elkner[at]ista[dot]ac[dot]at.

Before starting my PhD, I studied mathematics at the University of Vienna. There, my BSc thesis was supervised by Andreas Cap and my MSc thesis by Shantanu Dave.

I am interested in Lie theory and its interplay with algebraic and differential geometry. My PhD project is in geometric representation theory, with a focus on natural representation-theoretic symmetries. Using the recently introduced big algebras, I am studying such symmetries geometrically. Concretely, this involves working with affine Grassmannians, in particular their real forms and equivariant cohomology.
